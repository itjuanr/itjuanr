## About

Systems Requirements Analyst (Mid-level) at **Deloitte**, with a career that started in **test analysis** and evolved into requirements work for **critical industrial systems** in the mining sector. I have hands-on experience with **MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems)** — environments where a poorly written requirement isn't just a backlog problem, it's an operational risk.

That testing background still shapes how I work: I write specs with validation in mind from the start, not as a step added at the end. On top of the analysis side, I have working knowledge of **JavaScript and TypeScript** development, and I'm building my foundation in **Java** — which means I can have a grounded conversation with development teams, not just hand off a document and walk away.

- **Current role:** Systems Requirements Analyst · Mid-level · Deloitte
- **Domain:** MES integration and critical systems in the mining industry
- **Trajectory:** Test Analyst → Requirements Analyst
- **Development:** JavaScript · TypeScript · Java (in progress)
- **Education:** Post-graduate in Systems Development (Java) · Degree in Systems Analysis
